## Overview

The **Community Management Module** handles the lifecycle of digital communities and their membership in the banking platform.

It manages community creation, participant invitations, membership management, invitation responses, and community governance rules, integrating with CBA Adapter and Message Workers to ensure seamless community operations and member communications.
