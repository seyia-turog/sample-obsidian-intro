## Overview

The **Compliance Module** manages regulatory compliance data and customer information required for KYC, AML, and risk assessment in the digital banking platform.

It handles registered addresses, next of kin contacts, employment history, and emergency contacts, integrating with CBA Adapter and Message Workers to ensure proper compliance data management with validation, audit tracking, and user notifications.
