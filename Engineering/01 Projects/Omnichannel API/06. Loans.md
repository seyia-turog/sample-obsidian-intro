## Overview

The **Loans Module** handles credit applications, loan product discovery, and loan documentation in the digital banking platform.

It manages loan product eligibility, credit applications, loan calculations, application document uploads, guarantor and collateral details, and credit statements, integrating with CBA Adapter, Document Processor, and Payment Processor to ensure comprehensive loan application processing and management.
