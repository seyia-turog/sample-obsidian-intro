## Overview

The **Mandate Management Module** manages recurring payments and automated financial instructions initiated by users.

It handles creation, modification, retrieval, and deactivation of mandates, integrating with User Settings Utility to ensure secure, accurate, and compliant mandate lifecycle operations through user preferences.
