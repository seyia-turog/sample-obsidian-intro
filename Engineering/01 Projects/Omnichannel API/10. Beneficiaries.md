## Overview

The **Payment Beneficiaries Module** manages recipients for payments and transfers within the ADIBA ecosystem.  
It supports external and internal bank accounts, billers, and mobile wallet recipients, enabling streamlined and repeatable transactions.

The module integrates with Identity, CRM, CBA, Notification Workers, and User Settings Utilities to ensure secure, compliant, and auditable beneficiary management.
