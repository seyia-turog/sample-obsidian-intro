## Overview

The **Beneficiaries Management Module** handles the management of saved payment recipients in the digital banking platform.

It manages the creation and deletion of beneficiaries for external transfers, internal transfers, bill payments, and mobile wallet transfers, integrating with User Settings Utility to provide users with streamlined payment experiences through saved recipient details.
