## Overview

The **Savings Management Module** provides comprehensive operations for savings accounts within the ADIBA ecosystem.  
It covers account balances, transaction history, account creation, closure applications, preference configuration, and product retrieval.

The module integrates tightly with Identity, CRM, CBA systems, notification workers, document processors, and user settings utilities to ensure secure and seamless account management.
