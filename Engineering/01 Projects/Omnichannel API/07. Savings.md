## Overview

The **Savings Account Management Module** handles all savings account operations and balance inquiries in the digital banking platform.

It manages account creation, balance retrieval, transaction statements, account closure applications, account preferences, and product information, integrating with CBA Adapter and Message Workers to ensure accurate account management and timely customer notifications.
