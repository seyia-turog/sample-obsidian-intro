## Overview

The **Billers & Transaction Status Module** manages biller information and enables real-time tracking of payment transactions within the ADIBA ecosystem.  
It supports discovery of billers, their categories, payment items, and allows querying the status of transactions to provide users with up-to-date information.

The module integrates with Identity, CRM, CBA, and Notification Workers to ensure secure, verified, and accurate management of biller data and transaction status.
