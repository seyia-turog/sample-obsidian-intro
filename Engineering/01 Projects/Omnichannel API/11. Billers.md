## Overview

The **Billers Module** provides comprehensive biller discovery and bill payment information services in the digital banking platform.

It manages biller listings, biller-specific payment items, biller category organization, and transaction status queries, integrating with the Payment Processor to ensure users can discover and access all available bill payment services.
